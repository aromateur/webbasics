About Me

Hello! I'm Virginia and this is my website for Dr. Maceli's Info Tech class.

I'm an LIS graduate student interested in repatriation work and olfactory archives.

Some LIS issues close to my heart are:

  • Indigenous repatriation work (domestic and international)
  • Data privacy and the right to disappear
  • Working with IP-heavy subject matter
  • Archiving volatile materials
    • Inherently volatile materials
    • Materials made volatile through chemical exposure
  • Standardizing olfactory taxonomy
